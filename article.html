Data Story Telling : Something only which Mastermind's are aware of

Well, I heard this term Data Story Telling lately, but it caught my attention like a fighter jet breaking the sound barrier in the sky. We all need to know the deal-breaker for getting jobs. I have done a ton of research and found a way to speed up the process of Data Scientists getting hired as a fresher or as an entry-level in every startup or Tech Giants or FAANG whatever you call it. A Data Scientist collects the insights and displays it. In a nutshell, how he intelligibly explains is called Data Story Telling. It is important to be able to communicate data memorably. In addition to your skills, it is also really important to note this. Do you know how Spotify creates your playlist and how amazingly it provides it to you? Well, it gives you a notification in your mobile and even sends an email to you. This is a good way to communicate with you, right? Well, this a form of data storytelling. Now you would be able to get a clear picture and the importance of this topic. So I hope this data is useful for you and helps you grow in one or the other way. If you can't explain something in the way a novice could understand, the power of your work reduces significantly.

Data storytelling is about communicating your insights effectively, giving your data a voice. -Forbes
